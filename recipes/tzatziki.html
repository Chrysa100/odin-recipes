Tzatziki

Description

Greek tzatziki. A perfect sauce to accompany your meat

Ingredients

  • 1 large English cucumber, peeled and grated
  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • vinegar

Steps

  1. Sprinkle grated cucumber with 1/2 teaspoon salt in a bowl and let stand 10 to 15 minutes to draw out juice.
  2. Dump cucumber and its juice onto a sturdy, dry paper or cloth towel and squeeze as much moisture as possible from the cucumber.
  3. Place yogurt into a separate bowl. Mix cucumber into yogurt. Add garlic, cayenne pepper, and lemon juice; mix thoroughly.
  4. Stir dill into yogurt-cucumber mixture; season with salt and black pepper. Adjust all seasonings to taste.
